Cell Structure and Function Part II

prokaryotic and eukaryotic cells
similarities:
both have DNA
both have ribosomes

differences:
Eukaryotes have nucleus; prokaryotes do not
Eukaryotes have organelles; prokaryotes do not

plant and animal cells
similarities:
both have a nucleus
both have mitochondria

differences:
plants have chloroplasts; animals do not
animals have lysosomes; plants do not
